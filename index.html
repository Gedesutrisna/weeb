Learning Path

At Sekolah Coding, our Learning Path is designed to guide you through various stages of skill development in the tech industry. Whether you're a beginner or looking to deepen your expertise, we provide structured programs to help you achieve your career goals.

Web Development

Master front-end and back-end technologies to become a full-stack web developer. Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Node.js.

Data Science

Discover the power of data. Learn Python, machine learning, data analysis, and visualization to solve complex business problems with data-driven insights.

Mobile Development

Build iOS and Android apps with Swift, Kotlin, and Flutter. Learn how to create seamless mobile experiences for the growing smartphone market.

Cyber Security

Learn to protect systems and networks from cyber threats. This path covers ethical hacking, security protocols, and real-world case studies.
