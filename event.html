Web Development Workshop

Description

Discover a wide range of professional development courses designed to help you enhance your skills and advance your career in the tech industry. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your knowledge, we have courses tailored to your needs.

25 May, 2025 - 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM
Tech Innovation Hub, San Francisco
Organized by DevCon

Event Rundown

08:30 AM - Registration & Coffee

Participants arrive, register, and enjoy a welcome coffee before the event starts.

09:00 AM - Opening Ceremony & Keynote

The event officially begins with a keynote speech covering the latest trends in web development.

10:00 AM - Workshop: Laravel Basics

An introductory workshop on Laravel, covering installation, basic features, and framework architecture.

12:00 PM - Lunch Break

Enjoy a catered lunch and network with fellow attendees.

01:00 PM - Advanced Laravel Techniques

A deep dive into advanced features of Laravel, including Eloquent ORM, authentication, and middleware.

03:00 PM - Hands-on Coding Session

A live coding session where participants can practice building a web app using Laravel.

05:00 PM - Closing Remarks & Networking

The event wraps up with closing remarks, followed by an open networking session.
